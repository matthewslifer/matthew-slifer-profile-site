Electric Vehicles and Air Pollution

Project Link

For my capstone research project at UC Davis, my group and I investigated the impact that electric vehicle usage has on pollution levels for criteria pollutants. We looked at each county in California and compared pollution data provided by the California Air Resource Board. We then compared this to electric vehicle registration by county, with the data being provided by the California DMV. We found that counties that higher rate of EV usage were more likely to have lower pollution levels. We conducted this research by taking large data sets from our sources and running stastical analysis on the data of interest. The graphs were created using Microsoft Excel.

Fire Hazard and Vegetation in California

Project Link

This project compares fire risk and vegetation type in California. Data was pulled from the CalFire database. We used ArcGIS Pro to develop the map which involved implementing different layers of data. We also ran statiscal analysis to determine which vegetation types had the highest fire risk. This project was done during my undergraduate studies at UC Davis.
