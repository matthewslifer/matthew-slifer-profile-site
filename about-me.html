About Me

App Academy

Student, Since July 2022

San Francisco, CA

After graduating college, I started doing the typical job hunting in the field of Environmental Science. Quite frankly, I was discouraged by the limited number of positions available and was questioning my major choice. I sat down and evaluated what it was I wanted to do and where my interests were. I remembered in one of my classes, we used Python to build a function while working with some data. There was something satisfying and intriguing to me about typing something into a computer and having the code be executed. Not long after, I started taking a free online course in Python and knew immediately that I wanted to pursure a career in computer programming. I enrolled in App Academy to furthur develop, test, and perfect my skills. I am currently learning Python, SQL, HTML, JavaScript, and CSS. I am beyond excited to take the skills that I am learning and implement them in various ways to solve any problem and create a refined product.

College of Agricultural and Environmental Sciences

Research Associate, September 2021 to June 2022

Davis, California

While going to school in Davis, I wanted to intern for a position that would utilize my knowledge in Environmental Science while expanding my skills. I came across this position while taking a class with my wonderful professor, Gwen Arnold, when she was discussing that she was leading a research project. The project sought out to investigate the social and economic impact of hydraulic fracking. In this position, I compiled, evaluated, and performed statistical analysis through Microsoft Excel and Google Sheets. I also worked with an amazing team of interns to carry out these tasks. This position taught me how to critically and accurately perform data analysis. I am so grateful to have been able to work directly with one of my favorite Professors. The expertise I learned in this position is something that I could not have learned in school and I cannot wait to implement and improve upon these skills in future positions!

University of California, Davis

B.S. Environmental Science and Management, recieved June 2022

Davis, California

UC Davis is a world renowned public university offering a wide variety of studies with some of the best Professors in the United States. I pursued and received a B.S. in Environmental Science and Management with a concentration in Natural Resource Management. I am lucky enough to have had some of the best experts in the world in Environmental Science as my professors. In my coursework, I got to dive into Environmental Science while developing critical analysis skills, scientific data analysis skills, and complex problem solving. Going to Davis also expanded my view of the world. I had the opportunity to connect and work with people from all walks of life including people from different countries. I am humbled to have gotten to know, and learn, from these people. Going to UC Davis taught me how to tackle challenges, persevere through adversity, and how important it is to continually learn throughout life. Go Aggies!
